CEFR A2 "Can Do" Statements

Students at A2 Elementary level should be able to:

  • Understand main points in simple texts about familiar topics
  • Recognize and use common vocabulary for everyday situations
  • Hold simple conversations about personal information and daily activities
  • Write short, simple emails and descriptions
  • Use basic past, present, and future tenses correctly
  • Ask and answer common questions in everyday contexts
  • Understand the main points of conversations at normal speed
  • Use connecting words (and, but, because) to link simple sentences
üìå Remediation Strategies for Below 60%

Reading: Practice with children's stories and simple narratives. Focus on key words and main ideas.
Vocabulary: Use flashcards and word associations. Emphasize common household and school vocabulary.
Conversation: Conduct pair work. Practice dialogue patterns and responses to common questions.
Grammar: Review past tense, "to be," and simple connectors. Use controlled practice exercises.
Writing: Model emails and descriptions. Have students practice with guided outlines.
